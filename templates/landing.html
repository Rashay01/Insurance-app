Platinum Plus

Sanlam Platinum Plus is a insurance provider for top end Luxury and antique items.

A luxury item insurance policy may cover high-value items like jewelry, art, or electronics. Luxury items are considered elite in a particular society and are typically high-quality, rare, and require more resources to produce than non-luxury items. Examples of luxury items include:

  • Classic Cars
  • Yachts
  • High-end watches and jewelry
  • Private jets
  • Landscaping services
  • Expensive real estate
  • Country club memberships
  • Designer clothing and shoes
